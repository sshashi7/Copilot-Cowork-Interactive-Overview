1 Overview

What Is Copilot Cowork?

Copilot Cowork is Microsoft's agentic AI for M365 — it doesn't just answer questions, it does the work for you.

💡 The short version Copilot Chat is like asking a colleague a question. Copilot Cowork is like handing them a to-do list and coming back to find it all done — emails sent, decks built, meetings scheduled.
🚀

Microsoft 365 Copilot Frontier

Part of Microsoft 365 Copilot Frontier, Copilot Cowork introduces early agent‑style capabilities that can take actions across Microsoft 365.

🧠

Powered by Work IQ

The intelligence layer behind Cowork. Three components: Data (your M365 content), Memory (your work patterns), and Inference (anticipating what you need next).

🔀

Multi-Model Architecture

Copilot Cowork uses multiple large language models (including Anthropic models), selected and orchestrated by Microsoft to deliver the best experience for a given task..

Availability
🔑

Frontier Preview

Public preview via M365 Frontier preview program (since March 30, 2026). GA date TBA.

📋

Prerequisites

M365 Copilot (Premium) license + Frontier enrollment.

2 The Comparison

Copilot chat vs Cowork

This isn't a rebrand. It's a fundamental shift from "AI that assists you inside an app" to "AI that works for you across your entire M365 ecosystem."

Dimension Copilot Chat Copilot Cowork
Primary Mode Ask → Get an answer Ask → Get the work done
Scope App‑anchored, Graph‑grounded Cross-app — emails, calendar, files, chats, and workflow‑oriented
Behavior Reactive — you prompt, it responds Agentic — describe an outcome, it plans and executes multi-step workflows
M365 Integration Deep but contextual Deep and orchestrated
Background Work Requires you to stay in the conversation Queue tasks and come back later — works while you're away
Output In-app text responses and document creation End-to-end task completion — create docs, emails sent, meetings booked
Automation Manual or scheduled trigger every time Daily, Scheduled and Recurring Tasks
💡 Think of it this way Copilot Chat answers your questions. Copilot Cowork does your to-do list. One makes you faster — the other frees up your calendar.
3 How to Use

Getting Started

From setup to your first delegated task — here's how to start using Cowork in your daily workflow.

Setup (One-Time)
1

Open the M365 Copilot app

Go to m365.cloud.microsoft/chat and navigate to Agents in the left nav.

2

Find and add Cowork

Click All Agents, search "Cowork," select it, and pin it to your left rail.

3

Choose your model (optional)

Pick Claude 4.6, Opus 4.6, or Auto Mode (recommended — it selects the best model per task).

Daily Usage
4

Describe what you want done

Type naturally: Prep me for my 2pm with Contoso or Triage my inbox. Drag and drop files for extra context.

5

Watch it work — or don't

Cowork shows live progress. Steer mid-flight, queue more tasks, or walk away. It pauses for your approval before any outward action.

6

Review and approve

Before sending anything, Cowork shows exactly what it will do with a risk indicator. Approve, edit, or reject.

💡 Pro tip for prompting Be specific about what "done" looks like. Email John Smith a summary of the Contoso calls, include the timeline, cc my manager beats send John something about Contoso.

Custom Skills — Automate Your Recurring Work
💡 Build skills for the jobs you repeat Got a weekly status report, a monthly partner update, or a recurring customer prep routine? Turn it into a custom skill. Place a SKILL.md file in /Documents/Cowork/Skills/ on OneDrive — Cowork discovers it automatically. You can have up to 20 custom skills. Pair them with scheduled prompts to fully automate recurring work.
6 Gotchas & Tips

Watch Out For These

Cowork is powerful, but knowing its edges will save you time and prevent surprises.

⚠️ Always review before it sends Cowork pauses for approval before any outward action. Don't autopilot through — a quick glance prevents sending the wrong thing.
⚠️ Session memory doesn't carry over Cowork remembers everything within a session, but a new session starts fresh. Reference prior deliverables by name if needed.
⚠️ Be specific with names Use full names or aliases: email John Smith about Contoso. Saying just "email John" slows things down when your org has hundreds of Johns.
⚠️ Files must be in OneDrive/SharePoint Cowork only accesses OneDrive and SharePoint — not your local desktop. Upload first, or drag files into the chat.
⚠️ It can't delete SharePoint/OneDrive files Cowork can read, create, and edit — but cannot delete files from SharePoint or OneDrive. Do that manually.
⚠️ EU tenants require explicit opt-in EU Data Boundary tenants have Cowork off by default — your admin must explicitly enable Anthropic as a subprocessor.
💡 The trust equation Start with low-stakes tasks (inbox triage, meeting prep). Gradually hand off more complex work as you build confidence.
